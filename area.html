Getting around

Arrive, then relax.

Land at Entebbe and we'll have a driver ready — just share your flight details. Once you're settled, ride-hailing (Uber, Bolt & SafeBoda) is everywhere, special-hire taxis are easy to arrange at reception, and car hire can be organised on request.

  • Airport pickup & drop-off on request
  • Ride-hailing & taxis at the door
