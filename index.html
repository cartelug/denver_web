Welcome to Denver Elysium

A refined retreat, made for living well.

Denver Elysium is a collection of beautifully furnished apartments and residences tucked into Kakungulu Estates — one of Kampala's most sought-after addresses, just off Entebbe Road.

Every space is self-contained and styled for comfort: en-suite bathrooms, smart TVs, fast WiFi and warm, considered interiors. Downstairs, an indoor pool, rooftop lounge and fitness gym make every stay feel like an escape.
