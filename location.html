Find us

A sought-after address.

Denver Elysium sits within the leafy, gated calm of Kakungulu Estates in Akright City, Bwebajja — a quietly prestigious neighbourhood set back from the bustle yet only steps off Entebbe Road. Tree-lined avenues, secure estate roads and easy access to Lake Victoria make it one of the most desirable places to stay on the Entebbe corridor.

Whether you are arriving for business in Kampala, a getaway by the lake, or a comfortable stopover near the airport, you will find us perfectly placed — close to everything, removed from the noise.
