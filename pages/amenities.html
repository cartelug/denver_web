The sky lounge above it all.

Take the lift to the top and step into the open air. Our rooftop lounge frames sweeping views over Bwebajja and the distant shimmer of Lake Victoria — a setting made for sundowners, slow conversation and warm Ugandan evenings.

Plush seating, soft lighting and a full bar make it the natural gathering point of the property, whether you are toasting a milestone or watching the city lights flicker on.
