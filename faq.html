№ 01 — Booking & Payment

Booking, made simple.

Send us your dates through the booking form, on WhatsApp, or by email. A member of our team personally confirms availability and the best rate — usually within minutes, never more than a few hours.

No payment is taken online. You reserve your dates with us directly and we confirm the payment options when we hold your booking — simple, personal and secure.

Of course — plans change. Just let us know as early as you can and we'll do our very best to accommodate you. We're flexible and human about it.

Daily breakfast is included with the Two-Bedroom Apartment. For the Studio, breakfast can be added on request — just mention it in your booking notes.

№ 02 — Rooms & Comfort

In your apartment.

Completely. Every apartment is self-contained with an en-suite bathroom, smart TV, fridge, wardrobe and fast WiFi included as standard — plus full access to the indoor pool, gym and rooftop lounge.

The Studio is an intimate, self-contained space ideal for one or two guests (UGX 180,000/night). The Two-Bedroom is more generous — two bedrooms, a full living and dining area, and daily breakfast — perfect for families and longer stays (UGX 250,000/night).

Yes — regular housekeeping and fresh linen come as standard, and on-site laundry is available throughout your stay.

Absolutely. Free, fast WiFi runs throughout the property — work, stream and video-call without a hitch.

№ 03 — Arrival & Getting Around

Easy arrivals.

Standard check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can often be arranged — let us know your arrival time when booking and we'll do our best.

Yes. We're about 25 minutes from Entebbe International Airport and can arrange a pickup on request. Send us your flight details on WhatsApp and we'll have a driver ready.

Yes — free, secure parking is available on-site for guests with their own vehicle.

Ride-hailing (Uber, Bolt & SafeBoda) is everywhere and special-hire taxis are easy to arrange at reception. Car hire can also be organised on request. See our area guide for more.
