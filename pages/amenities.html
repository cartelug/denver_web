01 — Facilities

Designed for ease

A complete stay.

At Denver Elysium, nothing is an afterthought. We have gathered the finest comforts of a boutique resort under one roof in Akright City, Bwebajja — so the only thing left to plan is how you spend your time.

Indoor Pool

An all-weather swim.

Slip into our temperature-controlled indoor pool, sheltered from the equatorial sun and open from dawn until late. Floor-to-ceiling glazing washes the water in natural light while keeping every lap private and serene.

Whether you are easing into the morning or unwinding after a day in Kampala, the pool deck is your quiet retreat — towels, loungers and stillness always on hand.

Rooftop Lounge

The sky lounge above it all.

Take the lift to the top and step into the open air. Our rooftop lounge frames sweeping views over Bwebajja and the distant shimmer of Lake Victoria — a setting made for sundowners, slow conversation and warm Ugandan evenings.

Plush seating, soft lighting and a full bar make it the natural gathering point of the property, whether you are toasting a milestone or simply watching the city lights flicker on.

Fitness Gym

Keep your routine, anywhere.

Travel needn't interrupt your training. Our on-site fitness studio is fitted with modern cardio and strength equipment, climate-controlled and open around the clock so you can move on your own schedule.

Mirrored walls, fresh towels and chilled water keep every session crisp — a focused space to start the day strong or burn off the long haul.
